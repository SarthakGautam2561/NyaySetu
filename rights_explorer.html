🏠 Tenant Rights Overview

🛡️ Right to Fair Rent

gavel 📜 Rent Control Act, Section 4

Landlords cannot charge rent exceeding the 'standard rent' fixed by the Rent Control Court. You have the right to petition for rent fixation if you believe it is excessive.

lightbulb Tip: Always request rent receipts as proof of payment.

🚫 Protection from Eviction

gavel 📜 Transfer of Property Act

You cannot be forcefully evicted without due process of law. A valid legal notice must be served, and grounds for eviction must comply with state laws.

lightbulb Tip: Do not sign arbitrary agreements waving your statutory rights.
