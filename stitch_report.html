I. Ownership of Intellectual Assets

The employment contract dated Jan 2020 includes a standard IP assignment clause. However, Section 4.2 specifically excludes 'Independent Creations' developed without company resources. Analysis of git repositories suggests 92% of the logic was committed post-employment.

Strategic Note

Focus on the 'Clean Room' development methodology employed. If documented correctly, this serves as a complete defense against trade secret misappropriation.

II. Trade Secret Validity

The mathematical principles behind the algorithm are largely based on open-source Quantitative Finance libraries. Under the Public Domain Doctrine, generic mathematical optimizations cannot be claimed as proprietary secrets.
